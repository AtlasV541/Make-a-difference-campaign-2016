Horace Mann School Debate Team

Community Outreach Initiatives:

Horace Mann Debate has contributed to the Bronx Community. The Debate Team works with In-Tech Academy, a middle school in the Bronx, to teach and coach In-Tech Middle schoolers. Led by Ella Feiner, the initiative focuses on helping students break down debate topics and develop strong arguments. The debate team coaches them through various topics, helps them with research and practices public speaking drills.


Fun Facts about Debate:

  • Our Horace Mann Debate Team does Public Forum Debate which utilizess a traditional debate structure with two people per team.
  • Many famous individuals such as Oprah Winfrey, Adam Sandler, and Nelson Mandela all were on a Debate Team when they were in High School.
  • Public Forum generally Debates topics about foreign affairs, important national issues and domestic policy.
  • Public Forum Debate was First introduced in 2002.
